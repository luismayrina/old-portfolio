ABOUT ME

HELLO, I AM LUIS MAYRINA

Introduction

My name is Luis, and I'm a freelance web developer based in the Philippines. I develop and build user-centric digital products and interactive experiences to help people and brands achieve their goals. I assist organizations and clients in developing and launching products, improving user experience and product performance, or totally rebranding their identities.

As a designer and developer, I understand that the ideal user interface should be attractive and functional. I work with my clients to identify and solve challenges. In short, I create bolder online experiences.

How I started

The journey to becoming a freelance developer

Trying to understand and create a program used to be difficult for me. But having talented people in my environment have influenced myself to become better.

In 2021, I launched my very first website which was commissioned by a client, I was responsible for everything, which is the design, front-end development and back-end development of the public site as well as the areas hidden behind a login for customers.
